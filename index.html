are there unicode symbols for wmo clouds Yes, there are Unicode symbols for the WMO cloud types. Here are the Unicode codes for each of the main cloud types: Cirrus (Ci) - U+2601
Symbol: ☁️

Cirrostratus (Cs) - U+2601 with U+FE0F
Symbol: ☁️️

Cirrocumulus (Cc) - U+2601 with U+FE0E
Symbol: ☁️

Altostratus (As) - U+2601 with U+FE0E and U+1F301
Symbol: ☁️🌁

Altocumulus (Ac) - U+2601 with U+FE0E and U+1F300
Symbol: ☁️🌀

Stratus (St) - U+2601 with U+FE0F and U+1F301
Symbol: ☁️️🌁

Stratocumulus (Sc) - U+2601 with U+FE0F and U+1F300
Symbol: ☁️️🌀

Nimbostratus (Ns) - U+2601 with U+FE0E, U+2614, and U+FE0E
Symbol: ☁️☔️

Cumulus (Cu) - U+2601 with U+FE0E and U+1F300
Symbol: ☁️🌀

Cumulonimbus (Cb) - U+2601 with U+FE0E, U+26C8, and U+FE0E
Symbol: ☁️⛈️
